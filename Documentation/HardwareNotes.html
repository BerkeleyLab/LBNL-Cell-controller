If the EEBI relay opens because of a beam excursion beyond limits while beam current is above threshold the relay will remain open until the front panel push button is pressed.  If the relay opens for any other reason (such as an EEBI BPM clipping while beam current is above threshold or loss of BPM communication while beam current is above threshold) the relay will automatically reclose when the fault condition has cleared.

Test Points

There are four tip jacks for monitoring the power supply outputs.  The test points are protected against external short circuits by a 1kΩ series resistor.

Front Panel Controls

The front panel CFG/OPR toggle switch sets the network address mode.  In the CFG position pin 4 and 12 of the PMOD A connector (J13) are connected.  This sets the IPv4 network address to 192.168.1.127 and the Ethernet MAC address to AA:BB:CC:D5:E1:F2.  In the OPR position the pins are not connected and the Ethernet and IPv4 addresses are obtained from the flash memory.

The front panel RESET push button resets the errant electron beam interlock.
